WQI

Water Quality Index (WQI), Index that represents overall quality of water quality taken from such 5 main parameters as Dissolved Oxygen, BOD, Total Coliform Bacteria, Fecal Coliform Bacteria and Ammonia-Nitrogen.

  • Water Quality Standard

    • Score Meaning Mascot
    91-100 Very Good
    71-90 Good
    66-70 Nearly Good
    61-65 Fair
    51-60 Nearly Degenerate
    31-50 Degenerate
    0-30 Very Degenerate
