MWQI

Marine Water Quality Index (MWQI), Index that represents overall quality of marine water quality taken from such 8 main parameters as Temperature, Suspended Solid, pH, Dissolved Oxygen, Ammonia-Nitrogen, Nitrate-Nitrogen and Phosphate - Phosphorus.

  • Marine Water Quality Standard

    • Score Meaning Mascot
    90-100 Very Good
    80-90 Good
    50-80 Fair
    25-50 Degenerate
    0-25 Very Degenerate
